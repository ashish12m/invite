TRAVEL & ACCOMMODATIONS

The Grand Bahubali is nestled in the vibrant heart of Balotra, Rajasthan. To make your journey as seamless as possible, here are the best ways to reach us:

✈️ By Air: The nearest airport is Jodhpur Airport (JDH), located approximately 100 kilometers away. From Jodhpur, you can easily hire a taxi or catch a comfortable bus to Balotra, with departures available every 15 minutes from the major bus stands.

🚆 By Train: The nearest local railway station is Balotra Junction (BLT). Alternatively, Jodhpur Junction (JU) serves as a major railhead with extensive nationwide connections. If you are traveling from Jaipur or Delhi, taking a train is one of the most convenient and scenic options.

🏨 Accommodations: We are thrilled to host you! We will be providing comfortable accommodations for all our guests right at the venue. Simply pack your bags, and let us take care of the rest so we can focus on celebrating together in the serene Rajasthani landscape.
