FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Where will the wedding take place?

Yes! We're getting married at The Grand Bahubali (TGB) in Balotra, Rajasthan—a beautiful venue in the heart of the desert. We can't wait to share this experience with you.

Are kids welcome?

This is an adults-only (21+) celebration. We've designed the evening so that everyone can relax, unwind, and fully enjoy the festivities without little ones in tow.

What is the dress code?

Summer Formal. Think elegant and comfortable—sundresses, light suits, and breathable fabrics. Rajasthan evenings can be pleasant; we recommend bringing a light layer (shawl or blazer) for when the sun sets.

May I bring a guest?

Due to the intimate nature of our venue and space constraints, we ask that only those specifically named on your invitation attend. We wish we could celebrate with everyone!

What kind of food will be served?

Expect a rich menu with Indian and regional flavors—seasonal produce and dishes that celebrate the best of Rajasthani hospitality. We're excited to treat you to an unforgettable dining experience.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes! Shuttles will be available to transport guests to and from the venue. Specific pickup times and locations will be shared closer to the date.
